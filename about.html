Our Story

The luxury travel market has long been fragmented. Booking a private jet meant one broker, a yacht another, and a villa yet another. This created a web of separate calls, opaque pricing, and administrative headaches.

Founded in the spirit of efficiency, yan-testing launched BookLux to consolidate these world-class experiences into a single, intelligent interface. We realized that family offices, personal assistants, and high-net-worth individuals didn't need more vendors—they needed one trusted partner who could manage the entire ecosystem of luxury mobility and lifestyle.

Simplifying Complexity

Whether it's navigating the nuances of Mediterranean yacht charters, securing a private chef in a remote Alpine chalet, or arranging an empty-leg private jet on three hours' notice, we handle the complexity so you don't have to.
